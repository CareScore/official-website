CareScore FAQ

CareScore is an innovative online staff supervision model for care homes.

It uses self-assessments and supervisor assessments based on real-life work scenarios to identify strengths and weaknesses in care team performance. The assessments are sent via email and can be completed in less than 5 minutes.

CareScore generates instant personalised supervision reports with suggestions for improvement.

CareScore replaces time-consuming, face-to-face supervision with a quick and efficient online assessment. This helps care homes focus more on improving care delivery teams and the quality of care provided to residents.

CareScore helps prevent abuse, fosters a positive culture, provides strong leadership support, and offers valuable insights for care home managers and governance. It also helps care homes stay prepared for inspections and streamline employee management.

CareScore gathers data through self-assessments and supervisor assessments, which include real-life work scenarios and multiple-choice questions. The technology then compares the responses and generates performance ratings and insights, which are used to create action plans for care homes.

CareScore uses the data from assessments to generate personalised supervision reports, which supervisors can review and edit as needed. Frequent supervision sessions provide a more accurate picture of staff performance and allow for necessary improvements.

CareScore provides more accurate and effectively collected data, which helps staff perform better and improves care quality in care homes. It also promotes staff morale, accountability, and precise performance monitoring.
